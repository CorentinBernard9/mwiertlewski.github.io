About

The {{page.title}} studies the mechanics linked to tactile perception. Our approach lies at the intersection of the observation of tactile interaction with high precision devices, the design of haptic interfaces for reproducing realistic tactile sensations and the psychophysical study of human perception.

You can see some of our ongoing projects here

This group is part of the Delft Haptics lab of the Cognitive Robotics Department at the TU Delft
